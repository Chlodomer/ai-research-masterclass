About the Workshop

On April 16, 2026, dozens of Bar-Ilan researchers spent two and a half hours on a single question: how to use today's AI tools to move from a half-formed research idea to a complete proposal draft, without outsourcing the thinking. The workshop walked through four phases (writing a structured context brief, generating and verifying a bibliography, synthesizing an argument, and drafting a funded-ready proposal), each with specific prompts, guardrails, and a verification discipline. Everything on this page is the output: the prompts used, a live form for writing your own context brief, a verification checklist, and the full ISF proposal anatomy. Taken together, they contain every resource you need to reproduce the workshop's lessons on your own research.
