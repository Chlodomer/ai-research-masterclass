## How to Use This in the Workshop

1. **Module 1 (Bibliography):** Copy Parts 1-3 into your prompt when asking the AI to find literature. The terms, scholars, and boundaries make the search dramatically better.

2. **Module 2 (Context Building):** Upload your PDFs and paste this entire brief as the opening context. The AI now knows your vocabulary, your landscape, and your constraints.

3. **Module 3 (Proposal Generation):** Parts 4-5 become the seed for generating ISF-structured proposal sections. The AI writes a first pass; you evaluate and iterate.

_Spend 10-15 minutes on this before we begin. You already know the answers. The work is in making them explicit._
